## 🏁 Challenge <a id="challenge"></a>

The challenge consists of developing a **mobile application** that centralizes customer support through a **structured chat system**.

The journey begins with an **automated triage process**, which can be handled through a rule-based digital IVR (Interactive Voice Response) and frequently asked questions. After that, requests are routed to human agents organized into support groups and levels.

The system must store the entire interaction history and allow full tracking of the support flow, including ticket creation, escalation, and resolution.

The goal is to **optimize the support process**, improving operational efficiency, customer experience, and managerial control, with business rules refined collaboratively throughout development.

---

## 🎯 MVP (Minimum Viable Product) <a id="mvp"></a>

The solution to this challenge is **Orbita**, an innovative platform that combines a mobile application for customers and agents with a full web interface for administrators.

It centralizes customer support through a structured chat system, delivering a **smooth, organized, and efficient experience**.

The journey starts with a triage process based on business rules and FAQ-style questions, quickly directing users to the right support path.

Customers interact through a mobile app, while support agents can operate via mobile or web. Administrators have access to a robust web dashboard to manage queues, configure triage rules, monitor performance, and maintain full operational control.

Orbita automatically records the entire interaction history, allowing complete tracking of tickets from creation to escalation and closure.

What previously required multiple disconnected tools is now **centralized into a single platform with full traceability and control**.

This not only improves efficiency and reduces operational failures but also enhances customer experience and enables data-driven decision-making.

## 📊 Product Backlog <a id="product-backlog"></a>

| Rank | Priority | User Story | Sprint |
| :--: | :------: | :--------- | :----: |
| 1 | High | As an Administrator, I want to manage support groups and agents to organize ticket handling. | 1 |
| 2 | High | As a Customer, I want to start a support request to report an issue and receive assistance. | 1 |
| 3 | High | As a Customer, I want to view my tickets and their status to track my requests anytime. | 1 |
| 4 | High | As an Agent, I want to communicate with customers through a ticket chat to resolve issues. | 2 |
| 5 | Medium | As an Agent, I want to receive or pick up tickets to start handling customer requests. | 2 |
| 6 | Medium | As an Administrator, I want to configure triage rules to route tickets to the correct support groups. | 2 |
| 7 | Medium | As an Agent, I want to view full ticket history before responding. | 2 |
| 8 | Medium | As a Customer, I want to receive notifications about ticket updates. | 3 |
| 9 | Medium | As an Agent, I want to escalate tickets to other support groups when needed. | 3 |
| 10 | Low | As an Agent, I want to close a ticket after resolving the issue. | 2 |
| 11 | Low | As an Administrator, I want to view support KPIs to analyze performance and workload. | 3 |
